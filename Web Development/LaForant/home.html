LaForant is a tactical shooting game involving two teams with 6 players in each team. Every player can sign in and play remotely from anywhere in the world. Every game has 13 rounds and the team that wins 7 of them first winds the game. Players can choose their in-game characters called agents at the start of the game. Players can buy abilities and weapon at the start of the game.
